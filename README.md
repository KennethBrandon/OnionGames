# Onion Games

A collection of games written by my 11-year-old son.

## Games List

### [Soldier Showdown](BobCards.html)
A tactical wave warfare card game. Build your army, plan your waves, and defeat the enemy commander.

### [Shadow Chase](ShadowChase.html)
You are the light in a consuming darkness. Evade the shadow monster and survive as long as you can.

### [Lava Breaker](OnionBreaker.html)
Mustafar Edition. Help the Onion survive the heat in this intense breakout-style arcade game.

### [Tree Topper](ChristmasHopper.html)
Carry the star to the top of the Christmas tree! Jump on platforms, avoid the Grinch, and save the holiday.

### [Operation: Sleigh Ride](SantaSleigh.html)
Fly Santa's Sleigh, avoid the Grinches, and deliver Christmas to the finish line!

## How to Play

Open `index.html` in your web browser to see the game menu, or open the individual game files listed above.
