# Onion Games

A collection of games written by my 11-year-old son.

## Games List

### [The Grinch](GrinchGame/)
**Author:** EE
Silent Night, Sneaky Night. Steal presents, avoid traps, and ruin Christmas in this stealth puzzle game.

### [Operation: Sleigh Ride](SantaSleigh/)
**Author:** Onion
Fly Santa's Sleigh, avoid the Grinches, and deliver Christmas to the finish line!

### [Tree Topper](ChristmasHopper/)
**Author:** Onion
Carry the star to the top of the Christmas tree! Jump on platforms, avoid the Grinch, and save the holiday.

### [Onion Breaker](OnionBreaker/)
**Author:** Onion
Mustafar Edition. Help the Onion survive the heat in this intense breakout-style arcade game.

### [Shadow Chase](ShadowChase/)
**Author:** Onion
You are the light in a consuming darkness. Evade the shadow monster and survive as long as you can.

### [Soldier Showdown](BobCards/)
**Author:** Onion
A tactical wave warfare card game. Build your army, plan your waves, and defeat the enemy commander.

### [Sleigh Drop](SleighDrop/)
**Author:** DD
Guide Santa's sleigh and drop presents down chimneys! Avoid crashing into chimneys and deliver all the gifts to win.

### [Brick Eater](BrickEater/)
**Author:** DD
Eat Bricks, Breack Teeths

### [Christmas Cut](ChrismasCut/)
**Author:** B-Master
Physics puzzle fun! Cut the ropes to guide the star to the top of the tree before the snow buries everything.

### [Cursor Chase](CursorChase/)
**Author:** EE
Survive as long as you can! Guide your cursor to avoid the relentless swarm of monsters in this intense arcade challenge.

### [Tree Decorator](TreeDecorator/)
**Author:** EE
Get into the holiday spirit! Choose your tree, design custom ornaments, and light up your very own Christmas tree masterpiece.

## How to Play

Open `index.html` in your web browser to see the game menu, or navigate to the individual game folders listed above and open their `index.html`.
